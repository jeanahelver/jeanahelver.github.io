Xénos abiosis

silly me got reincarnated as a necromancer

The end is just the beginning; in more ways than one for me.
As I pass on, the life I had before already fades as a distant memory of a world that will be forgotten by time. Yet at the same instance as if no time has passed I snap wide awake. as if a nightmare has enveloped me, the blood dripping that was my face has vanished. My surroundings changed instantly from laying on concrete to standing in meadows.

Open fiction character that anyone can create content for.
Collaborative storytelling where multiple people can collaborate add too the cannon of the character and the world around them.
